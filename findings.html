Findings

  • Online education improves accessibility.
  • Learning can continue during disruptions.
  • Learners benefit from flexible schedules.
  • Data costs remain a major challenge.
  • Internet access affects educational success.
  • Digital inequality exists nationally.

Recommendations

Recommendation 1

Provide low-data educational content.

Recommendation 2

Provide learning materials in multiple South African languages.

Recommendation 3

Improve internet infrastructure.

Recommendation 4

Subsidise educational data.

Recommendation 5

Support blended learning approaches.

Conclusion

Online education has positively influenced access to learning at a national level, but challenges such as connectivity, affordability and engagement must still be addressed. A blended learning model offers the most balanced approach for future education.
